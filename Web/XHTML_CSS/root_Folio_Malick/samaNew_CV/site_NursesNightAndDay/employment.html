Job Opportunities

We, at Nurses Night And Day Inc. are constantly in seach for highly skilled professionals in the medical field while at the same time giving a chance to the new graduates through our intership program. We provide for those in need of mentoring a comprehensive in-house training.
We hire :

  • Registered Nurses,
  • Licensed Vocational Nurses,
  • Certified Aides,
  • Social Workers and
  • Therapists

We have appealing compensation packages and competitive salaries for our qualified applicants.

At Nurses Night And Day Inc., we understand that our greatest assets
are our employees and we know by experience that always giving
plenty of leeway to our staff members will accommodate their social life and thus make them more efficient in their performance.
So if your professional skills are caring for patients and you are dedicated to your work and you are longing for flexible working schedule, with minimum week-end or holiday shifts giving you a large amount of time
to spend with your own family, you've come to the right place.

We offer these benefits:

  • Paid Time Off
  • Medical Insurance
  • Dental Insurance
  • Lengthy Leave of Service
  • Medical and Dental Insurance
  • Mileage Reimbursement
  • Cell Phone
