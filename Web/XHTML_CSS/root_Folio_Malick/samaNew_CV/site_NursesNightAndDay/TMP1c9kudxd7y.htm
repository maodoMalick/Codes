The Personal Care You Need

While hospitals and nursing homes can provide the highest level of service, they often cannot provide the appropriate level of care, and, long-term institutional care can be isolating and frightening for the patient, as well as cost prohibitive for the family.

Being cared for at home, surrounded by family and friends, is often the best care alternative when acute care is no longer needed. The familiar environment is reassuring and uplifting to patients. But providing care at home without professional assistance can be overwhelming to families, and even dangerous to the patient.
