Making The Right Choice

Nurses Night and Day Inc. has been caring for patients in home settings for decades. Our experienced, interdisciplinary team of home care specialists will develop a plan of care that fulfills the physician's orders, provides the greatest amount of independance for the patient, and supports and educates family and friends.

 
Because we are:
  • Accredited by Joint Commission on Accreditation of Healthcare Organizations
  • Licensed by the State of Texas to provide Home and Community Support Services
  • Member of the Texas Association for Home Care
  • Certified for Medicaire and Medicaid programs

We also serve: Harris, Fort Bend, Galveston and Brazoria counties.


We at Nurses Night & Day Inc. believe that the home environment is essential to promoting wellness in the patient. Despite the challenges that can be present in these familiar surroundings, our aim is to help patients, their families and their friends identify and overcome these obstacles, thus enabling the patient to receive the highest quality care
at home.

Since 1984, we have worked to provide patient-centered care; hence we have incorporated the following goals to facilitate this process:

  • To protect and promote the rights of each patient in accordance with fundamental human, civil, constitutional, statutory rights.
  • To provide continuous and coordinated care in the proper setting that is individualized, adequate, effective and efficient.
  • To educate the patient, family and friends on how to facilitate the recovery and care process.
  • To maintain our organization's leadership ability to formulate and implement quality health care standards.
  • To manage organizational data with accuracy and confidentiality.
  • To foster a work environment that promotes self-development and continued learning.

The patient has the right to expect a quick response to reports of pain. At Nurses Night And Day Inc. we also subscribe to the concept of health as stated by the world health organization:
"Health is a state of maximal physical, mental and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity. The enjoyment of the highest attainable standard of health is one of the fundamental rights of every human being without distinction of race, religion, political belief, economic or social condition."
To the end, we are commited to providing the highest quality heath care.
